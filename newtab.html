Privacy Policy

Noir respects your privacy. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Data Storage

Your preferences and settings are stored in your browser's local storage.

Data Retention

You can clear all stored data by clearing your browser's cache or using the data aging feature in Smart Features.

Permissions

This extension only requests the minimum permissions needed to function as a new tab page.
